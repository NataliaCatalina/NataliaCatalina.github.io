What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence (otherwise known as emotional quotient or EQ) is the ability to consciously feel our emotions, and have a deep relationship with ourselves and other people. EQ has plenty of privileges in our life, some of the benefits of good management of EQ are the ability of awareness, and a better understanding and control of our emotions, surroundings and manage stress relief.
